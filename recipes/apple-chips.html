Apple Chips

Description

It's apples, it's chips, it's apple chips! Anything to get my ARFID child to start eating healthy snacks. We are going to pop these babies into the air fryer, shake it up a few times, and BAM! our kid is eating a healthy snack that gives him a satisfying crunch. Luckily we need very few ingredients to create this beauty.

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Cinnamon
  • Sugar
  • Cooking Spray

Steps

  1. Cut apple into thin slices.
  2. Sprinkle sugar and cinnamon on apple slices.
  3. Put in 300 degree air fryer for 15 min. and shake every 5 min.
  4. Set aside to cool and then enjoy!
